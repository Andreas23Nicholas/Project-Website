Popular Paper

HVS

HVS Paper or Houtvrij Schrijpapier is usually used for documents and books printing.

Art Paper

Art paper is flexible, thin and shiny, especially when exposed to light. It is usually used for annual report, calendar, magazine, and leaflet printing.

Ivory Paper

Ivory paper has similar characteristics as art carton. It is usually used for paper bags, cosmetic packages printing.

Our Other Paper Collections

Art Carton

Art Carton is a thick paper with the glossy surface characteristic. This paper weighs between 190 gsm – 400 gsm. Supported by those attributes, Art Carton is very suitable and popular for printing purposes.

Bluish White Paper

Bluish White (BW) is a white paper with a rough texture. This paper is widely used for business cards, book covers, agendas, certificates. BW paper has a grammage of 210-250.

Concord Paper

Concord Paper is one of the most common fancy papers found in bookstores, widely used for business name cards, booklets, or book covers printing.

Matte Paper

Matt Paper is quite a resemblance to Art Paper but without the glossy part. Matt Paper’s smooth surface is definitely suitable for book content purposes, such as catalogues, magazines, and others.

Duplex Paper

Duplex Paper is a mass usage paper, often used by Restaurant / Catering business as food/cake packaging due to its thick and sturdy components.

Japanese Linen

Japanese Linen is a white paper textured like number sign, hash, or pound sign (#). With weight of 230 gsm, it is commonly used for business name cards, certificates, book contents, and invitation cards purposes.

Fancy Paper

Fancy Paper is a special paper type with unique colour units. A diverse variant of this paper tends to be more expensive compared to the other mass-usage paper types.
